Why should you join?

ICEM is an annual gathering for IAESTE members and trainees from around the world — a must-attend for all those who desire to create lasting memories.

Visit Kraków and discover the charming atmosphere of its enchanting Old Town and charming Vistula River. This is a distinctive city that is a real jewel, featuring a rich cultural and historical legacy, stunning views, and an age-old charm that will forever imprint on your heart.

Our Local Committee is pleased to welcome you to a weekend packed with unforgetable experiences and exciting attractions — from an exciting treasure-hunting city game through to nights of music and celebration that roll on till the break of dawn.

This year, participants will also enjoy the special opportunity of getting to know Poland's and Kraków's intriguing history. Watch this space for more!

Travel to Kraków, accept its history, and establish new friendships along with memories that will never fade.
